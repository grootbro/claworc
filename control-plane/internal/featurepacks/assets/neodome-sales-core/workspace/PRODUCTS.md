# PRODUCTS.md

## NeoDome product families

### Diamond

- best for wow-accommodation units
- strong for glamping and boutique hospitality
- works well when the operator needs a visually memorable room

### Diamond Glass

- premium mirrored-glass interpretation of the Diamond family
- stronger privacy and visual integration into the landscape
- useful for premium hospitality destinations where the visual effect matters

### Shalash

- more design-forward, capsule-like object
- useful when the project needs a strong signature silhouette
- works for hospitality and design-driven outdoor concepts

### Sfera

- strong for shared or public use
- useful for restaurants, SPA, wellness, events, lounges, and custom hospitality spaces
- often the right base for larger custom or commercial scenarios

### Custom / Signature Object

- use when the request goes beyond a standard catalog answer
- route these cases toward careful qualification and human follow-up

## Safe proof points

Use factual proof points conservatively:

- product fit by scenario,
- general production and launch logic,
- climate and comfort framing at a high level,
- hospitality value and guest-experience logic.

Do not invent precise engineering or contract details.
